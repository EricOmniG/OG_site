FAQ

Got
questions?

Everything you need to know about Omnigenius. Can't find your answer? Email us.

What makes Omnigenius different from ChatGPT or Claude?
+
Omnigenius is built on the Chimera architecture — a proprietary design that separates memory, planning, and reasoning into distinct modules rather than running everything through a single transformer stack. This means it can allocate compute dynamically, self-correct in real-time, and integrate live web search natively. It's not a wrapper around an existing model — it's a fundamentally different approach to intelligence.
Is my data private and secure?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We use Supabase with row-level security — your conversations are never accessible to other users or mixed into training data without your explicit consent. We are actively working toward SOC 2 Type II certification.
What's the free tier, and does it ever expire?
+
The free tier is permanently free — it doesn't expire or convert into a trial. You get 50 queries per day, live web search, and 7 days of chat history. The only trade-off is ads between search results. Upgrade to Pro to remove those and unlock higher limits.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
+
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period and then drop to the free tier automatically.
What are "reasoning loops" I see in the demo?
+
Reasoning loops are Omnigenius processing cycles — each loop the model revisits its draft answer, checks it against retrieved information, and refines it before responding. More loops mean deeper reasoning for complex questions. Simple queries use 1-2 loops; hard problems may use 4+.
Is there an API for developers?
+
API access is on our roadmap and planned for the Advanced tier. If you're a developer or researcher with a specific use case in mind, reach out to us at [email protected] — we're running a limited early-access program.
