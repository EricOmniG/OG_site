Privacy Policy

Omnigenius LLC ("Omnigenius," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you use our services.

📅 Effective: March 5, 2026 📍 Jurisdiction: United States 🏢 Entity: Omnigenius LLC
💡 We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your conversations to train our models without explicit consent. This document explains exactly what we do with your information.

01 Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all products, services, and websites operated by Omnigenius LLC, including omnigenius.co and the Omnigenius API (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, you can contact us at any time at [email protected].

02 Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information — optional profile photo, username, and preferences you set in your account settings.
  • Payment information — billing details processed by Stripe. We never store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Conversations — messages, prompts, and content you submit to the Omnigenius AI.
  • Communications — emails or support messages you send to us.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, query types, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Log data — IP address, timestamps, referring URLs, and error logs.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies — as described in our Cookie Policy.

Information from Third Parties

  • OAuth providers — if you sign in with Google, GitHub, or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from that provider.
  • Analytics providers — aggregated usage statistics from analytics services.

04 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted third-party vendors who process data on our behalf (see Section 5). These parties are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Omnigenius, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — if Omnigenius LLC is acquired, merged, or undergoes a change of control, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With your consent — in any other circumstance where you have given explicit consent.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Conversation history — retained for 12 months by default. You can delete individual conversations or all history at any time from Settings.
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years as required by US tax and accounting laws.
  • Server logs — retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes.
  • Anonymized analytics — retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form.

07 Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard protections:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • Bcrypt password hashing — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Role-based access controls limiting employee access to user data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery, as required by applicable law.

08 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email or from Settings.
  • Do Not Sell — we do not sell your data. If our practices change, we will provide an explicit opt-out mechanism.

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.
