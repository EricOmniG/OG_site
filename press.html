About Omnigenius
Omnigenius is an AI company building a new kind of intelligence. Founded in 2025 by Eric Martin — a self-taught researcher who began development while working at an Amazon fulfillment center — Omnigenius is built on the ORCA architecture, a novel AI model design that is not derived from the transformer paradigm. Paired with a training methodology designed to mirror how humans learn — through structured tasks, feedback, and earned free exploration — the Omnigenius model is built for genuine reasoning, not pattern retrieval. Omnigenius is headquartered online and incorporated as Omnigenius LLC.
