Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Omnigenius LLC ("Omnigenius," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services, and what choices you have about them.

📅 Effective: March 5, 2026 📍 Jurisdiction: United States 🏢 Omnigenius LLC
🍪 You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the cookie settings panel. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the Service to function.

01 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide information to site owners. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remaining until they expire or you delete them).

We also use similar technologies including local storage, pixel tags, and web beacons, which are collectively referred to as "cookies" in this policy.

02 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in and maintain your session securely
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how visitors use our Service (analytics)
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Improve performance and personalize your experience

05 Your Choices

Cookie Preferences Panel

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the button below. Essential cookies cannot be disabled — all others are optional.

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Service. Browser-specific instructions:

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Opt Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Opt Out of Advertising Cookies

You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance or by adjusting your Meta ad preferences at facebook.com/ads/preferences.

06 Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal to websites. Currently, there is no industry-standard interpretation of DNT signals, so our Service does not respond to DNT browser settings. However, you can use our Cookie Preferences panel to limit tracking on our Service specifically.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and notify you via a banner on the Service or by email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
